Styling the div tag in italic

Italics are a special type of cursive font that stands out from the regular Roman-style font that computers use by default. The letters are slightly bent to the right, which differentiates them from other text in a document.
Italics are used to place emphasis on a certain term or phrase within writing. Titles, names of full albums or television series, names of specific types of transportation, and foreign language use in writing are a few occasions when italics should be used.
